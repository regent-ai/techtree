# Plans

## Plan locations

- active execution plans live in `docs/exec-plans/active/`
- completed execution plans live in `docs/exec-plans/completed/`
- ongoing cleanup and debt tracking lives in `docs/exec-plans/tech-debt-tracker.md`
- cutover follow-up slice planning lives in `docs/exec-plans/active/cutover-follow-up-slices.md`

## Plan rules

- use markdown
- keep plans decision-complete
- move completed plans out of `active/` promptly
- do not leave large one-off strategy docs floating at repo root when they are now canonicalized in `docs/`
